About Us

CookieMP is a new super light-weight multiplayer mod for Grand Theft Auto V, made for people who want to play the original not-modified game with their friends.

Our goal is keep game's originality and make it light-weight, simple to use and simple to develop a server!

Note that CookieMP is in early stage of development and it's not released yet. We will keep you updated with images and videos.
